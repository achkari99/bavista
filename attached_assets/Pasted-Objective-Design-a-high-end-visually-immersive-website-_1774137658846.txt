Objective:
Design a high-end, visually immersive website for a premium mocktail and event service brand (Mocktail by Bavista). The goal is to create a luxurious, emotionally engaging experience that convinces users to book the service for their events (weddings, parties, celebrations).

Design direction:

Luxury, elegant, and festive

Dark-mode dominant with glowing accents

Strong nightlife and celebration aesthetic

Visually rich, cinematic, and immersive

High-end event branding (not a simple café website)


Visual identity:

Primary colors:
- Deep black / dark background
- Gold accents (luxury)
- Warm neon glow (yellow/orange)

Accent colors:
- Vibrant cocktail colors (red, orange, blue, green)

Use:
- Glow effects
- Glass reflections
- Light gradients
- Subtle blur and depth (premium feel)

Typography:
- Elegant headings (slightly stylized or serif)
- Clean modern body text


Experience & interaction:

Highly immersive and interactive

Smooth cinematic transitions between sections

Hover effects on drinks:
- Glow + slight rotation + liquid shine

Micro-interactions:
- Light flickers
- Glow pulses
- Smooth scaling

Scroll animations:
- Reveal sections like a story
- Parallax with lights and drinks

Add subtle “event energy” through motion


Layout ideas:

Hero section:
- Full-screen dark background
- Cinematic video or image (event + drinks + lights)
- Minimal text:
  “Elevate your events”
  “Luxury mocktail experience”

- CTA: “Book your experience”

Experience section:
- Show events (weddings, parties, anniversaries)
- Emotional visuals (people enjoying)

Drinks showcase:
- Premium grid or carousel
- Each drink interactive (hover glow + animation)

Social proof:
- Real events photos/videos
- Testimonials

Booking section:
- Clear CTA (very important)
- “Reserve your event”


Architecture & quality:

Component-based architecture:
- Hero
- Experience showcase
- Drink cards
- Event gallery
- Booking CTA

Reusable animations and motion system

Clean and scalable structure


Creative freedom:

Think like a luxury event brand agency

Avoid generic restaurant layouts

Focus on emotional storytelling and atmosphere

Make users imagine their own event


Advanced ideas (IMPORTANT):

Add glowing neon elements

Use fire or light effects subtly (inspired by cocktails)

Floating elements (glasses, lights)

Animated background gradients (night vibes)

Add a “live event feeling” to the site


Evaluation criteria:

Luxury feel and premium perception

Emotional impact (does it make users want to book?)

Quality of motion and interaction

Originality and uniqueness


Goal:

Deliver a website that feels like a luxury event experience — not just a service page, but a visually immersive journey that sells emotion, celebration, and exclusivity.
